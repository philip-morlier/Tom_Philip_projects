By analyzing google trends, we can get an idea of how people are reacting (or not) to the changes involved with COVID-19. When people use a search engine such as Google, they are wanting to find more information on the topic. Therefore Google Trends is a good platform to gain insight on public interests and concerns, without the potential bias that is posed when conducting research questions.
